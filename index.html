How to Fold an Origami Squirtle:
Fold the body according to the directions above, but keep the arms larger.

Let's fold a Squirtle head and stomach:
Step 1: Fold your blue paper in half and then in half again. Use those folds as guidelines for cutting out 4 identical small squares. Each one will make a Squirtle face.
Step 2: Grab one of your mini squares and fold it in half two times. Open up your folds.
Step 3: Fold your corners into the center.
Step 4: Fold down the tips of your square.
